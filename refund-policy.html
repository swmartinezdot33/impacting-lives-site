Refund Policy

This Refund Policy applies to professional services, wellness memberships, and related fees billed by Impacting Lives LLC. It is intended to satisfy the transparency requirements of payment processors and communications partners while clearly explaining what portions of your charges may or may not be refundable.

1. General Principles

Impacting Lives LLC provides professional medical services, clinical consultations, and wellness programs. Fees are generally categorized as lab fees, consultation/professional fees, and membership or program fees. Because these services involve clinician time, third-party laboratories, and pharmacies, not all charges are refundable.

2. Laboratory Fees

  • Laboratory testing may be billed directly by the outside laboratory or passed through Impacting Lives LLC. Once a lab order has been processed or a specimen has been collected, associated lab fees are generally non-refundable.
  • If a lab order is cancelled before it is processed by the laboratory, we will work with the lab to determine whether a credit or refund is possible.
  • Any refund for lab fees is subject to the policies of the specific laboratory involved.

3. Consultation & Professional Fees

  • Consultation fees cover clinician review of your chart and labs, the telehealth visit itself, and care planning.
  • If you cancel at least 24 hours prior to the scheduled appointment, consultation charges are ordinarily eligible for refund or not billed, depending on the payment status at the time of cancellation.
  • Missed appointments or cancellations with less than 24 hoursâ€™ notice may be charged in full or subject to a no-show fee and are generally not refundable.

4. Memberships, Wellness Programs & Recurring Charges

  • Some services are offered through recurring memberships or wellness programs that include bundled access to consultations, monitoring, and support.
  • You may cancel a recurring membership at any time as described in our Cancellation Policy.
  • Charges already incurred for the current billing period are generally not refundable, as program resources and clinician availability have been allocated for that period.
  • In limited circumstances (for example, a documented billing error), we may issue a partial or full refund at our discretion.

5. Medications & Pharmacy Charges

Medications are dispensed by independent, licensed pharmacies. Pharmacy charges (including medication and shipping) are governed by the policies of those pharmacies and are not controlled by Impacting Lives LLC. As a result:

  • Questions or disputes about medication or shipping charges should be directed to the dispensing pharmacy.
  • We cannot typically refund pharmacy or shipping charges on behalf of the pharmacy.
