Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Impacting Lives LLC (“we,” “our,” or “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website and communication channels. It is intended to help satisfy transparency requirements of payment and communications vendors, including but not limited to Stripe, GoHighLevel, and Twilio.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to information collected through our public website and non-clinical communications (for example, general email or SMS alerts). Protected Health Information (PHI) associated with your medical care is handled through secure, HIPAA-supporting systems such as our electronic health record (EHR) and patient portal, and is governed by our HIPAA Notice of Privacy Practices.

2. Information We May Collect

  • Usage and device data: IP address, browser type, operating system, pages viewed, and time spent on pages.
  • Basic contact information: If you contact us by phone or email, we may receive your name, email address, phone number, and the content of your message.
  • Communications metadata: When we send permitted notifications or updates by SMS, voice, or email, we may collect delivery and engagement data (for example, whether a message was delivered or opened).

We ask that you avoid sending detailed medical or highly sensitive information by regular email or SMS; instead, use the Patient Portal for protected health communications.

3. How We Use Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve this website and our online presence
  • To respond to general inquiries about our services
  • To send permitted service-related messages, such as appointment reminders or account notifications, using communications platforms
  • To support security, fraud prevention, and compliance with applicable laws and regulations

4. Third-Party Service Providers

We may use third-party vendors to help operate this website and our communications, such as:

  • Payment processors (e.g., Stripe) to securely process card payments; we do not store full card numbers on this marketing site.
  • Customer relationship and marketing platforms (e.g., GoHighLevel) to manage leads and communications.
  • Communications providers (e.g., Twilio) to send SMS, voice, or email notifications where permitted.

These vendors are contractually expected to handle data in a secure manner and only for the purposes we authorize.

5. Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use cookies, pixels, or similar technologies to understand how visitors use the site and to improve performance. You can typically control cookies through your browser settings, though disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to help protect the information we collect via this website. No system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure, but we strive to follow industry-appropriate practices for data protection.

7. Your Choices

  • You may choose not to provide certain information, though this may limit your ability to use some features.
  • You may opt out of non-essential marketing communications by following the instructions in the message or contacting us directly.
